About G.V.H.S.S Madapally

English: G.V.H.S.S Madapally, established in 1924, is a government-run boys-only school managed by the Department of Education. Located in the rural region of Vatakara, Kozhikode, Kerala, the school offers education from grades 5 to 12 in Malayalam medium. It has 12 well-maintained classrooms, 2 staff rooms, a separate headmaster's office, and a pucca boundary wall. With 30 functional computers, a library of 20,000 books, a playground, and essential amenities like electricity and well water, the school also provides a midday meal prepared on campus. The academic year begins in April.
