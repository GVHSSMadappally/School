About Our Legacy

Founded in 1924, G.V.H.S.S Madapally stands as a beacon of academic prowess and holistic development in rural Kozhikode, Kerala. Managed by the Department of Education, our institution nurtures young minds from grade 5 through grade 12, delivering a robust Malayalam-medium curriculum enriched with modern amenities and innovative pedagogy.
